Our Community

A beautiful collaboration of hearts by aLoveSupreme and O’live.

By buying this soap you are supporting the work of an incredible women, Rosie Mashale, known affectionately as Mama Rosie. 100% proceeds of this heartfelt initiative will go to support Mama Rosie’s Children’s Home, Baphumelele.

The vision of Baphumelele is to provide shelter and early childhood care.

Baphumelele provides for vulnerable/orphaned children with chronic diseases and HIV/Aids in the Khayelithsa and surrounding areas. Baphumelele aims to impact these children’s lives in such a way that they will go on to make a difference in their society and beyond.
